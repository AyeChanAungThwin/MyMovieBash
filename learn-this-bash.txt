<1> You can search
	- by Name
	- by Month
	- by Year
	- by Director
	- by Film Production Company Name
	- by a piece of ANY word
	
	E.g., 
	Marvel <--- This will fetch all movies by Marvel.
	Iron Man <--- This will fetch all Iron Man movies.
	Cartoon <-- This will fetch all 2D/3D cartoons.
	Mockingjay <-- The movie name is "Hunger Games" and you know a piece of word.
